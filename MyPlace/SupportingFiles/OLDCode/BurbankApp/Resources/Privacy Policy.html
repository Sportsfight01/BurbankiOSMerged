When you use Burbank’s IT services, websites and/or applications, Burbank may obtain information using cookies, tags, web beacons, navigational data collection (such as log files, server logs and clickstream data) or other technologies to collect information including, but not limited to, the date, time, duration of visits and web pages accessed.

How you may complain about a breach of the Australian Privacy Principles and how Burbank Australia Pty Ltd will deal with such complaint
